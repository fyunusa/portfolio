Building LLMesh was born out of a simple frustration: PHP's ecosystem lacks a standardized, production-grade SDK for developing AI-integrated applications that matches the quality of Python's or TypeScript's frameworks. 

We needed a tool that was framework-agnostic, clean, and developer-friendly.

### Key Goals of LLMesh:
1. **Core SDK:** A minimal core package (`llmesh/core`) that has zero dependencies.
2. **Standard Interfaces:** Standardized APIs for Chat, Complete, Embed, Evals, Observability, and Cost Attribution.
3. **Laravel Support:** A first-class integration with Laravel that respects standard Laravel patterns.
